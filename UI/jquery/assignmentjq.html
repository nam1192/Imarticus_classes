Fruits of India:

India is the largest producer of Fruits in the world and is known as fruit basket of world. The national fruit of India is Mango.It is also called the King of fruits

India is second largest fruit producer in the world, There are large variety of fruits grown in different geographical zones in India.

In India, you can identify several seasonal fruits. Some of these fruits are readily available during the summer, while others are harvested and distributed primarily during the winter. Some fruits you can obtain as all-season fruits, which means they will be available all year. In this article, you will learn about the seasonal fruits in India and their benefits.
